Follow these steps to purchase a product using your credit card:

1. Use 4G or LTE Connection: Ensure you’re connected via 4G or LTE mobile data, not a VPN. Using a VPN can interfere with payment verification.

2. Select Your Product: Browse our available products and choose the one you’d like to purchase.

3. Add to Cart: Click on the product and add it to your cart.

4. Proceed to Checkout: When ready, go to your cart and click “Checkout.”

5. Pay as Guest: Select the “Pay as Guest” option at checkout—no account needed.

6. Choose the Right Country: Pick the country that matches your credit card’s billing address to ensure smooth processing.

7. Enter Payment Details: Provide your credit card information in the secure payment form.

8. Fill in Billing Address: Enter the exact billing address linked to your credit card. It must match perfectly for the transaction to succeed.

9. Keep the Amount Under 400€: Ensure your total order (including fees) is below 400€. Amounts above this may trigger 3D Secure authentication with your bank, slowing things down with extra steps.

10. Review Fees: We charge a 15% fee on all orders. For example, if your product is 100€, the total will be 85€ (100€ - 15€ fee). This fee supports our fast service.

11. Submit Payment: Double-check everything and click “Pay Now” to complete the purchase.

12. Wait for Processing: Most orders are processed within 30 minutes to 24 hours, depending on your bank’s speed.
